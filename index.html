    The Story Behind

    Hoot Quest

    More than 13 years ago, Hoot Quest began as a rough concept created by a college student with a passion for storytelling and game design.

    What started as the idea for a video game slowly evolved into something far larger — a dark fantasy world filled with heroes, monsters, mysteries, and survival.

    Now, after years of development, Hoot Quest is finally becoming reality.
