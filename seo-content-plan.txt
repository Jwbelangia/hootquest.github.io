HootQuest SEO Content Plan
=========================

Primary brand strategy
----------------------
Primary name: HootQuest
Alternate name: Hoot Quest

Rule:
- Use HootQuest as the main brand name in titles, headings, URLs, and structured data.
- Use Hoot Quest naturally in meta descriptions, FAQs, bios, alt text, and some social copy.
- Do not alternate randomly. Keep HootQuest dominant.

Keyword usage rules
-------------------
- Put the main keyword near the start of the title tag.
- Mention HootQuest in the H1.
- Mention Hoot Quest once in visible body copy where natural.
- Use one main keyword + two or three secondary keywords per page.
- Avoid stuffing long keyword lists into paragraphs.
- Write for users first, then search.

Bio and profile wording
-----------------------

Short brand bio:
- HootQuest is a cooperative fantasy board game from Playforge Entertainment where heroic owls defend Owlcrest against the Swarm of the Rat King.

Medium brand bio:
- HootQuest, also written as Hoot Quest, is a cooperative fantasy board game by Playforge Entertainment. Built around deck construction, dice rolling, grid movement, and campaign missions, the game sends players into Owlcrest to battle the Swarm of the Rat King.

Long brand bio:
- HootQuest is a cooperative fantasy board game by Playforge Entertainment that combines tactical card play, dice rolling, grid movement, and scenario-based campaign design. Set in Owlcrest, the game follows heroic owl defenders as they push back the rising threat of the Swarm of the Rat King. HootQuest is designed for players who enjoy cooperative strategy, character roles, modular encounters, and story-driven tabletop adventures.

Founder/company bio:
- Playforge Entertainment creates fantasy tabletop experiences focused on memorable worlds, tactical gameplay, and collectible characters. HootQuest is the studio’s flagship cooperative board game set in the world of Owlcrest.
